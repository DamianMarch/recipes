Jamaican Salmon with Coconut Cream Sauce

Description

Creamy and zingy new way of having salmon!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Mayonnaise
  • Caribbean jerk seasoning
  • Sour cream
  • Cream of coconut
  • Lime zest
  • Lime juice
  • Sweetened shredded coconut(toasted)

Steps

  1. Make the salmon
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Don't forget to sprinkle the coconut on top!
