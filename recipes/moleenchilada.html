Mole Enchilada

Description

Tasty mole enchiladas, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Mole(premade)
  • Chicken(or any protein or veggie, sweet potatoes work well too!)
  • Corn tortilla(flour tortilla if you prefer it)

Steps

  1. Make mole
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Heat tortilla on pan
  4. Assemble, bake!
